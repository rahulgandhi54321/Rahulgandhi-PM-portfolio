Job Hunter AI

Automating the most broken experience in tech — job hunting.

The Problem

Candidates spend 80% of time on repetitive admin — tailoring resumes, cold outreach, tracking applications. Zero intelligence matching them to the right roles. The emotional and time cost compounds every day.

The Solution

Full-stack AI platform: iOS app + Python backend. AI understands your profile, scans job boards, scores role fit, generates tailored outreach in seconds — not hours.

PM Role

Sole PM & builder, 0→1. Defined the problem space, designed iOS onboarding (Google Sign-In + profile), architected backend, shipped both repos to production. No team. No shortcuts.

PM Lens

Freemium model (same playbook → 30% free-to-paid lift at Invictus). Mobile-first (opportunities found on the go). Backend-first AI so core intelligence was solid before UI polish.
