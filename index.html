Case Study · AI Product · 0→1

Job Hunter AI

Automating the most broken experience in tech — job hunting.

The Problem

Job hunting is broken. Candidates spend 80% of their time on repetitive admin — tailoring resumes, tracking applications, cold outreach — and only 20% on what actually matters: preparing and connecting. There's no intelligent layer that truly understands your profile, matches you to the right roles, and personalizes outreach at scale.

The Solution

Job Hunter AI is a full-stack AI platform — iOS app + Python backend — that automates job discovery, role matching, and personalised outreach generation. AI understands the user's profile, scans job boards, scores role fit, and generates tailored application content in seconds, not hours.

My Role

As the sole PM & builder, I owned everything from 0→1: defining the problem space and user journey, designing the iOS onboarding experience (Google Sign-In, profile flow), architecting the backend API, and shipping both repos to production. Full PM + builder ownership — no team, no shortcuts.

Tech Stack

Swift (iOS)Python (Backend)Google Sign-In AI/MLREST APIsGitHub

PM Thinking

🎯
User Research Insight

Job seekers lose 5–8 hours/week on application admin. The emotional cost (rejection, uncertainty) is compounded by wasted effort on poor-fit roles.

📐
Product Decision

Chose iOS-first for the onboarding experience — mobile is where people discover opportunities. Backend-first AI to ensure the core intelligence was solid before UI polish.

💡
Monetization Vision

Freemium with premium AI features (personalized outreach, priority matching, recruiter insights) — same model that drove 30% free-to-paid lift at Invictus data.ai.
